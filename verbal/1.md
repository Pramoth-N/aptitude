

## 1. Sentence Correction

### Question
To create a truly successful movie, the director, the writer, the actors, and the cinematographer must work together with other people closely.

### Options
- corroborate  
- felicitate  
- ✅ **collaborate**  
- facilitate  

### Explanation

**Collaborate** means *work together*.  
Other options:

- Corroborate → confirm evidence ❌  
- Felicitate → congratulate ❌  
- Facilitate → make easier ❌  

Correct sentence:

> The director, writer, actors, and cinematographer must **collaborate** closely.
